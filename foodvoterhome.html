Welcome to Food Voter!

Welcome to Food Voter! Have you ever found yourself in a bind trying to organize where to go out to eat with your coworkers? Or maybe you have gone through the headache of figuring out what your family would like to eat for dinner? Food Voter is here to help! It's simple: login or signup, create a voting poll, invite friends, vote, and then get your results for the most popular choice of food that people would like to eat. Login or Sign Up below and start your voting!
